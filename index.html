About

Inspired by Southern Italy, Leuca is a neighborhood restaurant from Chef Andrew Carmellini's NoHo Hospitality Group, serving house-made pastas, wood-fired pizzas and rustic dishes in an elegant, sun-lit space.

Private Events

Leuca's private dining room accommodates up to 28 guests at a long, white marble dining table fully surrounded by hand-painted frescos inspire by the coastal Mediterranean. The room features its own cocktail bar, audio-visual and family-style menus by Chef Andrew Carmellini.
